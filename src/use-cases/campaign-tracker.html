Content calendar

Marketing teams lose track of what's going out, when, and who owns it — the plan is scattered across docs and the calendar is always a step behind. Here's how a team builds one shared calendar just by describing it, and keeps it current in plain language.

  • Built by chatting
  • The whole team can add
  • Everyone sees what's shipping

01 The problem

The calendar is always out of date

The plan lives in three different docs. One has the blog ideas, one has the email schedule, and the shared calendar hasn't been touched in weeks. Nobody's sure what's actually shipping this week.

The team doesn't want another tool to set up. They just want one list everyone can trust.
